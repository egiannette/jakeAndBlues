About

Jake & Blue's Raw Dog Food was founded 7 years ago. Our meat is a 3 way blend of beef, goat and lamb. Our grind includes 80% muscle meat, 15% organs and 5% fat including bone meal, trachea, and tripe. Our formula contains no grains, fillers, or by products. Our animals are healthy and range raised in either Oklahoma or Texas. The meat is processed in an FDA approved facility to ensure the highest quality for your furry friend.
